The Museum of Cool Stuff

Welcome to the Museum of Cool Stuff!

The Wizard Exhibit

Visit the wizard exhibit, and the exhibits of its wizardry! On display, we have an assortment of crystal balls, a true Emerald Tablet, our own Philosopher's Stone, and explanations of magic forces that act upon them. Through the work of our curators, you too can behold the powers of magic just as ancient wizards may have long ago.

With our display of crystal balls, you can learn of our understanding of the magic of wizards from premodern to modern times. Learn of the many powers invested within them, and the many possibilities their use brought throughout time.

Discover how the Emerald Tablet was really created, and how its usage brought the Philosopher's Stone for display today. With the work of our curators, we have come to be able to tell a story of their creation and their nature as artifacts.
