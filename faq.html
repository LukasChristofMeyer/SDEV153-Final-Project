Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Where do you get your artifacts?

    A: We take them from the public domain from the Smithsonian and Wikimedia.

  • Q: I think I might die I don't drink the holy grail. Can I drink from the holy grail?

    A: No.

  • Q: What does your activated crystal ball perdict about the stock market?

    A: The crystal ball was activated before the 1929 Wall Street stock market crash, so it apparently only says that you should sell everything.

  • Q: Can I buy any of your artifacts?

    A: No, only we can buy our artifacts!

  • Q: Hello, I'm a scientist! Can I take a closer look at your artifacts?

    A: Yes! Go to our website at https://lukaschristofmeyer.github.io/SDEV153-Final-Project/, and feel free to look closer at the pictures.

  • Q: Where are you physically located?

    A: Ask GitHub.

  • Q: Do wizards actually exist?

    A: Maybe!

  • Q: Are your artifacts real?

    A: No, none of our artifacts are Latin-American currency.

  • Q: I found a cool thing, can we dedicate it to your museum?

    A: The Museum of Cool Stuff only carries stuff, not things.

  • Q: Does the Holy Grail actually give immortality?

    A: Yes, and stop asking.
